Salad

Description

Some veg to complement the gnocchi and garlic bread. Best served with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Ingredients

  • Bag of mixed leaf salad
  • One carrot
  • Pack of Feta cheese
  • One red pepper
  • Bunch of spring onion

Method

  1. Grate carrot.
  2. Chop red pepper.
  3. Chop spring onion.
  4. Chop feta into cubes.
  5. Mix all chopped veg with mixed leaf salad and serve.

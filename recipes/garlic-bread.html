Garlic Bread

Descripton

Garlic, butter and toasted bread. A great accompaniment to Gnocchi.

Ingredients

  • A nice bit of bread or baguette
  • Four garlic cloves
  • A healthy amount of butter (more than you think you need)

Method

  1. Melt butter in a pan over a low heat.
  2. Peel and crush garlic.
  3. Add garlic to melted butter and stir well.
  4. Chop bread or baguette into individual portions.
  5. Spoon butter-garlic mix onto sliced bread.
  6. Pop under the grill until cooked then serve.

Gnocchi

Description

Simple and quick tomato, chorizo and cheese based Gnocchi dish by Miguel Barclay.

Ingredients

  • Tin of Chopped Toms
  • 200g Chorizo
  • Bag of Gnocchi
  • Wedge of Blue Cheese
  • Salt
  • Ground Pepper

Method

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200C
  2. Empty bag of gnocchi into an oven safe dish.
  3. Add tinned tomatoes.
  4. Chop and add chorizo.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Stir well and put in oven for 20 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle blue cheese over the top.
  8. Return to oven for 10 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven, serve.
